Cope — Disclaimer

Please read this before using Cope.

Not a Medical or Mental Health Service

Cope is a self-guided wellness tool designed to offer grounding exercises, affirmations, and daily emotional check-ins. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care, therapy, counseling, or medical treatment. Cope does not diagnose, treat, or provide clinical advice for any mental health condition.

If You're in Crisis

Cope is not equipped to handle emergencies or crisis situations. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm, or suicidal ideation, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988 in the US) or go to your nearest emergency room immediately.

In an emergency, always contact local emergency services (911 in the US) or a licensed professional. Cope is a supplementary wellness tool, not an emergency resource.

No Guarantee of Outcomes

Individual results from using Cope will vary. We make no guarantees regarding specific emotional, psychological, or wellness outcomes from use of the app.

Consult a Professional

If you are currently experiencing symptoms of a mental health condition, or have questions about your mental health, please consult a licensed therapist, counselor, or physician. Cope is designed to complement, not replace, professional care.

Data & Privacy

Any personal reflections, entries, or check-ins you provide within Cope are used solely to power the app's features. We do not sell personal wellness data to third parties.
