Our Story

An IT operator turned AI builder.

Toastid Tech, LLC was founded in 2026 by Sean Sziklas Sr., after a 20+ year career managing operations at organizations like USGS and DIRECTV. That background — helpdesk management, technical escalation, real-time analysis — shapes how every product gets built: direct, practical, and focused on solving the actual problem in front of the user.

Every app in the Toastid suite is designed, coded, and shipped by that same operator, with a small freelance team supporting design, web, and compliance work.
